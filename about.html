About Allure Microblading

Where Artistry Meets Expertise

Allure Microblading was founded in 2016 with a singular purpose: to offer Auckland clients cosmetic tattoo results that are refined, natural-looking, and built to last. Since opening, the studio has earned the trust of the New Zealand community through consistent results, a commitment to hygiene, and an honest, client-first consultation process.

Jessica is a certified cosmetic tattoo artist and PMU specialist, holding certification from California, USA, and a cosmetic tattoo licence in New Zealand, with international training spanning the Americas, Europe, and Asia. She has studied advanced micropigmentation and colour theory under leading PMU educators and industry masters, completed masterclasses in precision hair-stroke work and pigment science, and trained in feathering techniques across both classic manual and machine methods. Continuous professional development is central to her practice and she attends industry seminars and advanced masterclasses every year to remain at the cutting edge of technique, pigment science, and skin theory. Jessica works exclusively with state-of-the-art devices and the finest products available on the market, and holds herself to the highest hygiene standards for every client. Her dedication to quality and care has earned the trust of New Zealand clients for nearly a decade, reflected in her consistently five-star reviews.
