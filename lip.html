Colour Correction

Lip Neutralisation

A corrective treatment designed to tone down darker or uneven lip pigmentation before applying your desired colour. Neutralisation balances the undertone reducing dark, purple, or brown tones, so the final lip blush result is more vibrant and true to colour. Please note that naturally very dark or near-black lip tones may require laser lightening prior to treatment, as tattooing alone cannot remove deep natural pigmentation. We recommend a consultation to assess your lip colour and set realistic expectations.
